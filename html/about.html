ABOUT US











Our Story

Vera, the beloved bag and shoe business, was founded by entrepreneur Chandrè Leigh Davids in 2023. Chandrè Leigh Davids, a fashion enthusiast with a passion for sustainability, had always been frustrated by the lack of high-quality, eco-friendly accessories on the market. After years of researching and experimenting with innovative materials, Chandrè finally launched Vera, with a debut collection of handcrafted bags made from repurposed plastic bottles and recycled fabric. The brand quickly gained traction among fashion-conscious consumers who shared Chandrè commitment to sustainability. As Vera's popularity grew, Chandrè expanded her product line to include stylish shoes made from recycled rubber and plant-based materials. Today, Vera is a go-to destination for stylish and eco-friendly accessories, with a loyal following of customers who appreciate the brand's commitment to reducing waste and promoting sustainability.




We use durable material

Vera, a bag and shoe business deeply committed to sustainability, prioritizes eco-friendly materials throughout its operations. By meticulously sourcing materials from sustainable suppliers, Vera ensures its products have minimal negative impact on the environment. This includes utilizing renewable resources like organic cotton, bamboo, hemp, and recycled fabrics, as well as incorporating recycled materials such as polyester and nylon. Additionally, Vera opts for natural and biodegradable materials like cork and sustainably harvested wood to reduce pollution and waste accumulation. With a focus on reducing chemical usage and adhering to certifications for organic materials and fair trade practices, Vera maintains transparency and accountability in its sustainability efforts. Through these practices, Vera not only aligns with its values of environmental stewardship and social responsibility but also meets the growing demand from consumers for eco-friendly alternatives in bags and shoes, strengthening its brand reputation and fostering long-term relationships with environmentally conscious consumers.

Mission Statement: "At Vera, we are dedicated to revolutionizing the way we think about fashion and waste. We believe that beauty and sustainability are not mutually exclusive, and that our products should not only make you look great, but also help to preserve our planet for future generations.
